What makes it different

Built for how
students actually live

01
🃏
Pop-Out Button

Press the side button and your cards fan out instantly. An angled internal ramp lifts your cards so you can see and pick whichever one you need — no digging around.

02
🔒
Retention Lip Lock

Cards won't slip out on their own. Each slot has a precision retention lip — cards need a deliberate press to clear it. Safe in bags, pockets, anywhere.

03
🧲
Magnetic Phone Snap

Four embedded disc magnets on the back let KARDO snap securely to your phone or phone case. Works on any flat-backed device — no MagSafe required.

04
🪢
Neck Lanyard Ready

A reinforced lanyard hole at the top means you can wear KARDO around your neck — perfect for school events, labs, or anywhere your hands need to be free.

05
🎨
4 Colour Options

Choose from four clean colours — Black, White, Red, or Blue. Each unit is printed fresh to your order in your chosen colour.

06
💸
Student Priced

Step by step

How KARDO works

01
📱
Snap to Phone

Hold KARDO near the back of your phone. The 4 disc magnets snap it on instantly — secure, flat, and barely noticeable.

→
02
👆
Press the Button

When you need a card, press the side button. The internal ramp fans your cards upward so you can see all of them at once.

→
03
🃏
Pick Your Card

Each card is visible and grippable. The retention lips hold all others in place while you remove just the one you need.

→
04
✅
Snap Back & Go

Slide your card back in — it clicks past the retention lip and stays secure. You're done. Walk away. KARDO handled it.
